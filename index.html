It uses natural language processing (NLP) techniques to analyze the content of both the resume and the job description, providing users with an ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score that indicates how well their resume matches the requirements of the job. This tool helps job seekers optimize their resumes for better chances of passing through ATS filters and getting noticed by recruiters.
